1. Almost 99% of the time, the right time is right now.
   > 把握此次此刻，因为会变成永远的彼时彼刻
2. No one is impressed with your possessions as you are.
   > 有少许的人会在意你所拥有的，但是哪些更更在意你所在意的，才是志同道合的。打动人的永远是你的细节和品质。
3. Dont ever work for someone you dont want to become.
   > 工作的目的在于提成自己，成为自己想成为的那个人。
4. Cultivate 12 people who love you, because they are worth more than 12 millions people who like you.
   > 关注身边的人
5. Dont keep making the same mistakes; try to make new mistakes.
   > 不要停滞不前，发现问题要改正，找到一个机制避免下次发生。
6. If you stop to listen to a musician or stree performer for more than a minute, you owe them a dolloer
   > 对于别人的任何一个优点，都要大声表示肯定。
7. Anything you say before the word "but" does not count.
   > 听清楚别人表达的重点是什么。
8. When you forgive others, they may not notice, but you will heal. Forgiveness is not something we do for others, it is a gift to ourselves.
   > 宽恕别人，自己得到益处。
9. Courtesy costs nothing. Lower the toilet seat after use. Let the people in the elevator exit before you enter. Return shopping carts to their designated areas. When you borrow something, return it better shape(filed up, cleaned) than when you got it. 
   > 生活中的点点滴滴，都是修行。
10. Whenever there is an argument between two sides, find the third side.
   > 永远不要把自己放置在二元对立中。
11. Efficiency is highly overrated; Goofing off is highly underrated. Regularly scheduled sabbaths, sabbaticals, vacations, breaks, aimless walks and time off are essential for top performance of any kind. The best work ethic requires a good rest ethic.
   > 学会休息，是高效工作的前提。千万别burn-out
12. When you lead, you real job is to create more leaders, not more followers.
   > 成为领导的目的在于insipre 其他人也能lead
13. Criticize in private, praise in public.
   > 私下批评，公开表扬
14. Life lessons will be presented to you in the order they are needed. Everything you need to master the lesson is within you. Once you have learned a lesson, you will be presented with next one. If you are alive, that means you still have lessons to learn.
   > 生命中的课程总是不早不晚的到来，到来的时候。到来的挑战，你已经有足够的能力去应对。课程接着课程，挑战接着挑战，不断来临，直到生命终结。
15. It is the duty of a student to get everything out of a teacher. and the duty of a techer to get everything out of a student.
   > 不懂就要询问，准备接受所有的询问，这就是成为学生和老师的真谛。
16. If winning becomes too important in a game, change the rules to make it more fun. Changing rules can becomes the new game.
   > 游戏在于过程。如果游戏结果重要，可以改变游戏规则，变成游戏过程更加好玩。
17. Ask funders for money, and they give you advice; but ask for advice and they give you money.
   > 寻求建议，资源便会到来。
18. Productivity is often a distraction. Dont amin for better ways to get through your task as quickly as possible, rather aim for better tasks that you never want to stop doing.
   > 追求一种能够享受的任务而不是其他
19. Immediately pay what you owe to vendors, workers, contractors. They will go out of their way to work with you first next time.
   > 该付给别人的一定要立即支付。
20. The biggest lie we tell ourselves is "I dont need to write this down because I will remember it".
   > 永远不要相信自己的记忆
21. You growth as a conscious being is measured by the number of uncomfortable conversations you are willing to have.
   > 个人的成长是有那些不舒服的对话来衡量的。
22. Speak confidently as if you are right, but listen carefully as if you are wrong.
   > 自信说话，细心聆听。
23. Handy measure: the distance between your fingertips of your outstretched arms at shoulder level is your height.
   > 用身边的工具解决问题
24. The consistency of your endeavors (exercise, companionship, work) is more important than the quantity. Nothing beats small things done every day, which is way more important than what you do occasionally.
   > 习惯的力量是无穷的。每天进步一点点。
25. Making art is not selfish; it's for the rest of us. If you don't do your thing, you are cheating us.
   > 做该做的事情。对他人有意义的事情。
26. Never ask a women if she is pregnant. Let her tell if she it.
   > 不要询问女士的生理状况，包括年龄，体重，手术等。
27. Three things you need: The ability to not give up something till it works, the ability to give up something that does not work, and the trust in other people to help you distinguish between the two.
   > 三样东西同等重要：坚持直到成功，放弃不起作用的事情，信任他们和自我的智慧去分辨。
28. When publich speaking, pause frequently. Pause beore you say something in a new way, pause after you have said something you beieve is important, and pause as a relief to let listeners absortb details.
   > 公开演讲的能力在于合适的停顿。在于引出新的知识，在于结束信息，在于说完你觉得重要的信息。
29. There is no such thing as being 'on time'. You are either late or you are early. You choose.
   > 没有恰到好处的合适时间，要么早，要么晚。这都在于你的选择。（个人认为意识早些，行动晚些）
30. Ask anyone you admire: Their lucky breaks happened on a detour from their main goal. So embrace detours. Life is not a straight line for anyone.
   > 拥抱曲折，生活从来不是一条直线。 问一下你倾佩的人，他们的幸运往往发生在曲折的路上。
31. The best way to get a correct answer on the internet is to post an obviously wrong answer and wait for someone to correct you.
   > 在得到正确答案之前，或许有很多恶意，请忽略他们。
32. You'll get 10X better results by elevating good behaviro rather than punishing bad behavior, especially in children and animals.
   > 鼓励好的行为，比惩罚坏的行为有10倍的效益。
33. Spend as much time crafting the subject line of an email as the message itself because the subject line is often the only thing people read.
   > 邮件的标题重要性怎么强调都不过分。最好再有一个概要或是tldr，用1234罗列要点。
34. Don't wait for the strom to pass, dance in the rain.
   > 学会大雨中跳舞，这是暴风雨中最重要的，也是最简单的。
35. When checking references for a job application, employers may be reluctant or prohibited from saying anything negative, so leave or send a message that says, "Get back to me if you highly recommend this applicant as super great" If they don't reply take that as a negative.
   > 如何避免让别人说出负面的话，却能得到自己想要的信息。
36. Use a password manager; Safe, easiler, better.
   > 请使用密码管理器。
37. Half the skill of being educated is learning what you can ignore.
   > 学会什么东西可以忽略，是一个受教育的标志。
38. The advantage of a rediculously ambitious goal is that it sets the bar very high so even in failure it may be a success measured by the ordinary.
   > 取法乎上，仅得乎中
39. A great way to understand yourself is to seriouly reflect on everyting you find irritating in others.
   > 认真反思自己让别人讨厌的事情，这样才能有自我认识。
